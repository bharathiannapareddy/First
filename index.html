Veena Chawla (Journey from Manav to Mahamanav, Jiva to Brahmasrishti)

Veena ji was born in Mata ji ki Kutiya, Anandpur Kurukshetra, Haryana in the evening of November 27, 1964
in a religious family. At the time of naming, it was predicted that she would be on path of god realisation.
She is the youngest among four siblings. She got married in the Chawla family and her husband is a retired
government employee. He has two sons and two daughters-in-law.




Veena Ji did M.A. (Hindi) and B. Ed. After getting education, since 1994, she is working as Vice Principal and Hindi teacher in
Nirmal Public School, Karnal. Veena ji's nature has always been truthful. You have been interested in watching and listening
to Sri Krishna Leelas since your childhood. In 1992, she received Shri Krishna Mahamantri from Shri Krishna Pranami Mandir, Model Town, Karnal.

You worked selflessly and considering work as worship in the field of household and education. By understanding the will of
the Lord and having full faith in the Lord, she faced the joys and sorrows that come in life.On February 21, 2013, at the age
of forty-eight, suddenly got your self-realization i.e. self-realization. On February 26-27, that is, on the sixth day, your soul crossed
Kshar brahma, Akshar brahm i.e. gross, subtle, Mahakaran and met Shri Krishna, Sachidanand, Parabrahma, Purna Parmatma in Maharas. After meeting, the soul found its identity in the form of Brahmasrishti. The vastness of the supreme element Shri Krishna, the mystery of the soul, the creation of the universe and the body became acquainted with the mysteries of the universe and are continuously becoming so. By supreme grace, a simple mind touched Mahamati. With this the prediction made in childhood proved to be true.
